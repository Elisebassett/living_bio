About Living Biographies

We believe everyone has a story to tell, we just provide the venue!

Living Biographies is a story telling community event based in Salt Lake City Utah. Each month, Living Biographies features a different member of the community with a story to tell.

Members of the community are invited to come, listen, and ask questions.

Each month we record the event and put it on our website for our podcast listeners.

Shanny Boyce: Living in the Silence

Shanny Boyce is a 24 year old professional from South Africa. She got her degree in psychology and sociology from Duke, is currently finishing her master’s in social engineering of body behavior. She works for some of the top law firms in the state, on some of the most prominent cases in the news today.

Shanny is full of fascinating stories, from swimming with sharks as a kid in africa, to being a human lie detector. The one she has chosen to speak about is the provcess of going completely deaf. One of the few changes in her life she was able to prepare for.

Sariah Stapp: We Walk for Love

In February of 2014, Sariah was a single working professional who had an idea. She wanted to walk the length of California, boarder to boarder, to spread the most important message of life, “love thyself.”

She recruited her friend Bekah Beachley and together they accomplished that goal. Their journey began in Brookings, Oregon, they walked the 1,027 miles to the Mexico border. It was a life changing experience and they want to share it with the world.
